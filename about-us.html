Established as a dedicated food service distribution division of Binzagr Company in 2007, Binzagr Professional Solutions works as a fast-paced caterer of more than 1000 products, mainly food supplements, condiments, and cleaning/sanitizing materials. With a hard-line policy on valuing customer trust and product quality, we act as the first-hand buyer or direct recipient from renowned national and international manufacturers, and thus deliver a diverse and disruptive array of products directly to the customer.

An incessant passion to live up to the evolving trends and transformations in the modern market has ever enabled us to bring topmost brands in the world to your hands. We love to live with our never-closing enthusiastic eyes glued to the global trade hub to see ‘What’s new?’, or ‘Is there something exciting?’.

With a goodwill built up over 140 years since the inception in 1881 by virtue of its steer dedication and austere commitment in serving the best to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia through multifarious channels, Binzagr is a popular name everywhere in the country. Nourishing and nurturing this rich and proud legacy being far important than anything, the Professional Solutions segment of the company is always on a relentless strive to fulfil the likes and tastes of the food service industries in the Kingdom. The world is on wheels with us carrying a wide range of highly sought-after products in the domestic and foreign markets to meet the needs of the small and big HORECA entities.

It is a matter of fact that the world we live is endless, and is abound in endless possibilities with innumerable gateways wide open for trailblazing explorations and experiments. Binzagr Professional Solutions is fervently gearing up for such remarkable innovations to go beyond boundaries, and get for you all you want.
